May 2020 PollScout

I am a moderate user of Twitter. One of the biggest reasons for being on twitter is the love for the free flowing knowledge by industry leaders and influencers. Another biggest reason is, Twitter Polls. They are funny, informative and engaging.

But finding interesting Twitter polls is difficult❗

Hence I thought of building PollScout app to help me and (hopefully) everyone to bookmark interesting polls and make them discoverable.

For tracking a poll, just reply to any Poll tweet with @pollscout track #hashtag1 #hashtag2
